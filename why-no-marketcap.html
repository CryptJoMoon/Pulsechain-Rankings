Why is there no Market Cap or Rank?

If you're seeing a token without a market cap or global rank, don't worry — it's common, especially for new or smaller tokens.

1. No Circulating Supply Provided

CoinGecko needs the circulating supply to calculate market cap. If the project hasn’t submitted this info, it can’t be shown.

2. New or Illiquid Token

If a token is newly listed or only trades on a small DEX with low volume, CoinGecko may delay market cap/rank visibility.

3. Missing or Incomplete Metadata

If the contract, supply, or team info is incomplete or misconfigured, CoinGecko may hide ranking temporarily.

4. Wrapped, Derivative, or Pegged Assets

Tokens that represent other assets (e.g. staked or wrapped tokens) may not get separate rankings.

5. Under Manual Review

CoinGecko sometimes reviews tokens before assigning a market cap/rank — especially if it's new or flagged for spam/scam checks.

If you're the token team, you can submit fixes using the CoinGecko Request Form.
